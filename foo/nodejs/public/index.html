Food Paradise

Food Paradise is a leading food ordering and delivery startup in Mathikere,Bangalore. The company started operations in 2019 and is headquartered in Mathkere,Bengaluru.

Food Pradise works by acting as a bridge between customers and restaurants. It utilizes an innovative technology platform that allows customers to order food from nearby restaurants and get it delivered at their doorstep
